About Aadhira Primetech

Built for people who want to actually use what they learn.

A small‑town institute teaching the same enterprise systems that bigger cities charge a premium for — alongside school coaching for Class 6 to 12.

Aadhira Primetech started with a simple idea: where you study shouldn't decide what you're allowed to learn. We teach SAP FICO, SAP MM, full‑stack development and data analytics — the same enterprise systems run by large companies — right here on GST Road in Acharapakkam, instead of asking students to travel into the city for it.

Every professional course is built around real project work rather than theory alone. You leave with something to show — an internship certificate tied to an actual project — not just a completion slip.

Alongside our IT batches, we also run a school coaching wing for Class 6 to 12: Maths & Science, Bio Maths, Business Maths, and Accountancy, taught with the same attention to fundamentals as our professional courses.

Whether you join us at the centre or log in from home, the syllabus, the project work, and the support are the same — and our office stays open 9 AM to 9 PM, every day, for exactly that reason: so you can reach us when it suits you.

  • 100% job assistance after course completion
  • Internship certificate for every project‑based course
  • Small batches, hands‑on system access
  • Online and offline — your choice, same syllabus
  • School coaching and IT training under one roof
  • Open 9 AM – 9 PM, every day of the week
